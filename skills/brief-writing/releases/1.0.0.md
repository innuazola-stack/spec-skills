# brief-writing 1.0.0

## Status

Released.

## Summary

Initial sealed release of the `brief-writing` skill for writing professional Simplified Chinese product, design, technical, and hybrid briefs for human decision makers.

## Included

- `SKILL.md` with workflow, brief logic, hard rules, and common mistakes.
- `references/brief-methodology.md` with 14 external methodology sources and distilled writing logic.
- `references/brief-output-contract.md` with structure options, evidence rules, blocking gates, scoring rubric, and review checklist.
- `assets/Brief.template.md` with a reusable Chinese brief template.
- `agents/openai.yaml` UI metadata.
- `tools/validate_skill_static.py` static release validator.
- `tools/build_release.py` deterministic release artifact builder.

## Quality Bar

- Professional, formal Simplified Chinese output.
- Human-readable final-view documents only.
- Revision table first for saved or approval-bound briefs.
- Source-backed claims and reference round trip.
- Purpose-scoped content with no unrelated or redundant sections.
- Fit-for-purpose use of prose, tables, Mermaid, and LaTeX.
