# prd-writing 1.0.0

## Summary

Seals `prd-writing` as a production-ready writing skill for creating and reviewing professional Simplified Chinese software PRDs.

## Quality Bar

- Final PRDs must begin with revision history.
- Product facts must be source-backed.
- Methodology references must be cited in the body and cannot be the sole support for product facts.
- Requirements must be prioritized, traceable, and linked to acceptance criteria.
- Saved Markdown PRDs must pass `tools/validate_prd_document.py` before delivery.
- Invalid PRDs must be revised or reported as blocked instead of being delivered as ready.
